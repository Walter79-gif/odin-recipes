Chicken Salad recipe

A Photo of Chicken Salad

Chicken salad preparation is easy and simple

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Place almonds in a frying pan.
  2. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently.
  3. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.
  4. In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper.
  5. Toss with chicken, almonds, and celery.

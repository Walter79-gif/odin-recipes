Coffee Recipe

Special Coffee

Special coffee that every one likes drinking as they relax with their loved ones.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound coarsely ground coffee beans
  • 4 ½ cups cold water
  • cheesecloth
  • coffee filters

Steps

  1. Put coffee grounds in a large container. Slowly pour water over the grounds.
  2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and steep at room temperature for 18 to 24 hours.
  3. Line a strainer with several layers of cheesecloth and place atop a pitcher; strain coffee through the cheesecloth into the pitcher.
  4. Discard the grounds. To get a clearer brew, strain coffee again through coffee filters. Store in refrigerator.

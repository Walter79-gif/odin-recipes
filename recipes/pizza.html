Hummus Pizza Recipe

Hummus Pizza

Hummus pizza is the most loved food in my residence. We like eating pizza during outings and on special occations.

Ingredients

  • 10 ounce refrigerated pizza crush dough
  • One cup hummus spread
  • 1Â½ cups sliced bell peppers, any color.
  • 1 cup broccoli florets.
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese.

Steps

Step 1

  1. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees C (220 degrees C).

Step 2

  1. Roll out pizza crust and place on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  2. Spread a thin layer of hummus over the crust.
  3. Arrange sliced peppers and broccoli over the hummus, and top with shredded cheese.

Step 3

  1. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown.
  2. Cheese is melted in the center, 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Slice and serve.
